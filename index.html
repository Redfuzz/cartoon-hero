BAYMAX

About Baymax

An inflatable robot whose only purpose is to take of people. After his creators death, Baymax was inherited by Tadashi's brother Hiro. Who both became members of the superhero team Big Hero 6.

Baymax's characteristics

  1. Instantly summoned by the sound of distress.
  2. Extremely intelligent.
  3. He is selfless, nurturing, and devoted to his patients.
